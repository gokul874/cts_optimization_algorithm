Key Optimization Criteria
High Rating Priority

Providers with higher CMS ratings (1-5) are prioritized to ensure quality care for members.

Low Cost Optimization

Among high-rated providers, those with lower costs are selected to optimize network expenses.

Shortest Distance

When rating and cost are equal, the nearest provider is chosen for member convenience.
