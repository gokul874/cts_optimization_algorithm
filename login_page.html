Our Services

We provide comprehensive healthcare services and solutions to meet diverse needs

Primary Services

  • Medicare: Federal health insurance for people 65+, certain younger people with disabilities, and those with End-Stage Renal Disease.
  • Medicaid: Joint federal and state program for low-income individuals and families.
  • Children's Health Insurance Program (CHIP): Health insurance for children from families with low or moderate income.
  • Health Insurance Marketplaces: Manages federal and state marketplaces under the Affordable Care Act.

Types of Services

  • Health insurance coverage (Medicare, Medicaid, CHIP)
  • Provider services and payment management
  • Regulatory oversight of healthcare quality and safety
  • Health policy implementation and research
